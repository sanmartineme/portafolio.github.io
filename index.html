About me

I am a Product Designer and UX Designer with more than 8 years of experience in the financial industry, where I have integrated high-performance multidisciplinary teams. My design approach is user-centered, applying design thinking methodologies to understand their needs and behaviors.

From initial research to implementation, I follow an iterative process that includes prototyping, testing, and adjustments based on real feedback.

I have a solid ability to negotiate and reach agreements with business, product and engineering areas, which allows me to align objectives and maximize results.

With a data-driven approach, I use metrics and analysis to inform my design decisions, always with the goal of creating experiences that positively impact the business.
